Orchard House

Orchard House resides in the heart of the East Essex countryside. It has been owned by Sean's grandparents, Roy & Thelma Raven, since 1966 and remains a working orchard to this day.
Sean & Fay are both honoured to be celebrating their special day in the family's orchard.

By Car

Use the postcode SS4 3RJ in your Sat Nav. If you come from Rochford on the Ashingdon Road, the road begins as Brays Lane and turns into Apton Hall Road after a mile or so.
It's on the right and can appear quite quickly as it is a fast road. Look out for the signs for ‘Sean & Fay’s wedding’ and ‘Orchard House’
Parking will be available; please follow the signs upon arrival.

By Train

The nearest station is Rochford (on the Southend Victoria line) which is a 7 minute taxi ride away. There is a taxi rank but we advise booking in advance. Trains run from London Liverpool Street and take 50 minutes. Alternatively, you can take the C2C line which runs from London Fenchurch Street and get off at Southend Central. Orchard House is then a 25 minute taxi drive.

Taxis
